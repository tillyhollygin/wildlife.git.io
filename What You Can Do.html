It is easy to dissociate from the effects of Climate Change.

It is even easier to make a few changes and help the planet out!



ACTION REASON
Use Reusable Coffee Cups This saves single-use plastic waste and saves you money as many coffee shops will give you a discount!
Support a Charity You could adopt an endangered animal through WWF or volunteer or fund-raise for an environmental charity like GREENPEACE
Avoid Palm Oil Palm Oil production is fueling deforestation. Try to avoid products that contain it to reduce the demand and inform yourself at Saynotopalmoil.com
